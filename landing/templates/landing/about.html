About

Have you ever wanted to easily find nearby concerts? The purpose of this website is to do just that! MyConcerts is a tool that allows users to easily search for concerts in their vicinity.

We provide Spotify support as well! By linking to a spotify account, MyConcerts can provide recommendations directly from your favorite artists and music on spotify!

Have an particular artist in mind? You can easily search their name and be presented with any concerts that might be related. We provide additional filters for Genre, City, and Time-frame!

A concert's details page shows info ranging from date and venue locations to price and even a sample of an artist's music. If you would like to purchase a ticket, we provide a link to the Ticketmaster website as well.

In addition, if there is a specific concert that piques your interest, you can easily "favorite" that concert and then filter by favorites to find it quickly.

This app was made as for a school project. As concert-enjoyers, we were inspired to design a website that made it easy to find nearby concerts. The developers are all Texas A&M computer science students. Thank you for using our software and enjoy!
